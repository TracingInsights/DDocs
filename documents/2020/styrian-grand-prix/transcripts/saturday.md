It was not easy and I tried to be a bit close to the guy, I had to try to stay in his tracks to have a little bit less water. But anyway I think P2 is still good. It’s just very tricky out there and of course the last lap would have been better. I don’t think it was enough to beat Lewis but the pace overall was good.Q: We heard you talking about visibility, was that on your visor or was it just the sheer spray coming up? In the dry I think we can have a good shot at it. We’ll see how it’s going to go. I think our car, in general, is better than last week’s so I’m looking forward to it.Q: Lewis, that was a dominant lap on the last attempt.
