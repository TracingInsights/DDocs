F1 - 2025 Belgian Grand Prix - Friday Press Conference Transcript

And then, of course, what these two weeks have been about is really trying to meet as many people as possible from the team. There is no other way, I think, to try to get to know the team, to start to understand the strengths and the weaknesses, to understand how we will be able to eventually support. That’s what I’ve been doing the last two weeks, trying to meet as many of our people as possible. And the good news is it’s an incredible team. They didn’t win by luck or by mistakes. They won because of the accumulation of talents that there is in the buildings. Every small box you open and you look at how they go about a given topic or another thing, every time you say, “Wow, that’s really nice.” And that’s all coming from the people there at all levels that have been doing amazing jobs for all these years. Like I said earlier, there’s a great energy in the team. We’re moving forwards.
