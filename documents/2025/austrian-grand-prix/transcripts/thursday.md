**Q:** (Josh Suttill – The Race) On the topic of the racing rules, a question for all three of you. Do you think F1's racing rules are overregulated at the moment? Are you happy with how they are, and how much are you actually thinking about them when you're racing at a circuit like this?

Obviously, there are still degrees of factors that can’t really be written down. Every situation is different, but I think it at least gives us as drivers some clarity on what is and isn’t allowed. It’s just important that people don’t treat them as black and white—this is what needs to happen, this is what isn’t going to happen—because even if you wrote 10 pages of lines and a driver’s going to find a grey area just from the situation you end up in.
